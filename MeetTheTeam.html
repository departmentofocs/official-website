Quantitative

Matt Dulansky

He is a fourth year student working under The Synstitute on the collaboration efforts of D.O.C.S with local fishermen. He works with the team to facilitate marine conservation efforts throughout San Diego. Particularly he focuses on the impact of the Seaport development through Protea development, and its impact on the few surviving commercial fishermen of San Diego.

Operations

Sarah Hollister

Sarah is a fourth-year student working at The Synstitute as part of the collaborative efforts between D.O.C.S and local seafood restaurants. She plays a crucial role in working with her team to engage with local seafood restaurants and promote marine conservation initiatives in San Diego. Sarah's dedication to marine life conservation drives her efforts to connect the fishing community with local restaurants and raise awareness about the impacts of these developments on the region's marine ecosystem.
